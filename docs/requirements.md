---

This version requires **PHP 8.4+** and **Laravel 11 or higher**.

If you're on an older version of PHP or Laravel, take a look at [v4](https://docs.spatie.be/laravel-activitylog/v4), [v3](https://docs.spatie.be/laravel-activitylog/v3), [v2](https://docs.spatie.be/laravel-activitylog/v2) or [v1](https://docs.spatie.be/laravel-activitylog/v1) of this package.
